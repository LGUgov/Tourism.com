NARUKBOS BURGOS

Mandate

he Local Government of Burgos acknowledges and recognizes the authority, function and importance of local governance as mandated by the Constitution of the Philippines which is further supported by the Local Government Code (RA7160), in the delivery of responsive, accessible, courteous, and effective public service.

Vision

We, the people of Burgos, envision our Municipality as an Agro-Tourism in Western Pangasinan, inhabited by God-fearing, loving, healthy and self-reliant citizenry living in clean, peaceful, orderly, safe and Disaster-free environment

Mission

Our vision will be achieved through the delivery of responsive and holistic development programs by competent and responsive public servants, pursuing massive and sustainable crop, livestock and poultry production and promotion of Eco-Tourism to uplift the living condition of the townspeople.
