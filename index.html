about us

The Local Government of Burgos acknowledges and recognizes the authority, function and importance of local governance as mandated by the Constitution of the Philippines which is further supported by the Local Government Code (RA7160), in the delivery of responsive, accessible, courteous, and effective public service. It further recognizes the power and responsibility of the LGU to protect its citizens, preserve its environment, enhance tourism and culture, promote health and safety and ensure that basic services are delivered in a regular and timely manner.
